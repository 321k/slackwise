SlackWise - The TransferWise Slack bot

The TransferWise Slack bot lets you manage your TransferWise account from Slack.

Command

Description

Example

/transferwise personal_token
This is how you connect your TransferWise account.
/transferwise asdf89238yd234362u13fwt43f
/latest
Shows the lastest activity on your account
/balances
Shows your account balances
/pay email amount currency
Creates a payment for Erik for 100 EUR. The command returns a link you can use to approve the payment.
/pay erik.edin@transferwise.com 100 EUR
/homecurrency currency
Changes your home currency to any other allowed currency. By default, your home currency is the currency you have the most money in. Leave the currency blank to see your active home currency.
/homecurrency EUR
/switch-profile
If you have a business profile, you can use this command to switch between your personal and business profile.
/transferwise-bot-feedback feedback
Send feedback about the bot
/transferwise-bot-feedback please add more features
