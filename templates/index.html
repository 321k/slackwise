The TransferWise Slack bot lets you manage your TransferWise account from Slack.

  • Connecting your TransferWise account
  • Latest activity
  • Account balances
  • Pay someone
  • Change your home currency
  • Switch between profiles
  • Send feedback
  • Privacy policy

Connect your TransferWise account

/transferwise
Once you have added the TransferWise bot to Slack, type in /transferwise on Slack to connect to TransferWise. You will get a link back that lets you connect your TransferWise account. You have to do this before any other commands will work.

Latest activity

/latest
Use /latest to see the lastest transfers and activity.

See your balances

/balances
/balances shows your TransferWise account balances.

Pay someone

/pay email amount currency
/pay firstname.lastname@gmail.com 100 EUR creates a payment for 100 EUR. The command returns a link you can use to complete the payment.

Change your home currency

/home-currency currency
/home-currency EUR changes your home currency to any other allowed currency. By default, your home currency is the currency you have the most money in. Leave the currency blank to see your active home currency.

Switch between profiles

/switch-profile
If you have a business profile, you can use this /switch-profile to switch between your personal and business profile.

Give feedback

/transferwise-bot-feedback feedback

Send feedback about the bot. /transferwise-bot-feedback please add more features
